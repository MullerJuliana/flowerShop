About us:

Flower paradise is a small flower shop based in san jose california. We pride ourselves on two things. affordability and uniqueness. We find rare flowers such as ghost orchids which could be found in south florida and cuba, corpse flowers that are found natively to rainforests of western sumatra and indonesia. And much more.

Highlights:

Oregon’s portland rose festival is a famous annual parade, where they purchased more than a thousand roses from us.

A bouquet of lilies of the valley and ghost orchids purchased by American movie star, Josh hutcherson.

Our 2021 succulent fundraiser dedicated to the conservation of cacti and succulent species raised 50,000 dollars.
