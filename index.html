About us:

Flower paradise is a small flower shop based in san jose california. We pride ourselves on two things. affordability and uniqueness. We find rare flowers such as ghost orchids which could be found in south florida and cuba, corpse flowers that are found natively to rainforests of western sumatra and indonesia. And much more.
